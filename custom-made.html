Custom-Made Process

1. The first step of alchemy: your inspiration / wish / story / memory is generated, and the idea of alchemy is born

2. Collision alchemy formula: make an appointment with the designer for collision inspiration and pay a deposit. In this step, you can tell the designer your story and requirements (such as aesthetic preference, budget, favorite gem material, etc.), and if you don't have specific ideas, you can also trust the designer's specialty and let us design it.

3. Selection and formulation: we will take about 5 working days to integrate the inspiration according to your story and requirements, find the corresponding theme, jewelry material and technology, and build the design prototype

4. Alchemy formula molding: we will further confirm the scheme with you, and we will polish the design scheme together

5. The formula is further refined: the modeling starts, and the collision imagination begins to land in this step. In this step, the designer will communicate with experienced technicians to help us realize our ideas and find the perfect integration point between precision technology, materials and ideal goals.

6. Furnace forging: jewelry making, the product of the fusion of consciousness and matter is about to appear. (according to the single or complex materials and the difficulty of production process, it usually takes 20-35 days for gold materials and 30-90 days for gem inlays.)

7. Alchemy completion: the combination of material and spirit is born. After the final payment is delivered, you will have customized jewelry with exclusive spiritual energy.
